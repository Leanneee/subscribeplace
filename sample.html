Pricing Breakdown

At SubscribePlace, any person or group can setup a profile for free. And you can cancel anytime.

Profile owners set their monthly subscription price for subscribers to view their profile content. Then, SubscribePlace adds a service fee on top of the subscription price to pay for credit card processing and to keep our platform running at its best.

The pricing table is below.

Subscription PriceSubscribePlace Service Fee *
$10 or less$1.99
$11 - $20$2.99
$21 & above15% of subscription price
* Profile owners must make a $200 minimumIf a Profile Owner generates more than $200 in a calendar month from Active Subscribers as measured on the last day of the calendar month, then that profile owner will have met the $200 minimum threshold.  If a profile owner meets or exceeds the $200 minimum threshold, then the Hosting Fee related to the $200 minimum will be waived.  However, any profile that generates less than the $200 minimum threshold from active subscribers as measured on the last day of the calendar month shall be subject to a Hosting Fee of $200 per month.  The $200 Hosting Fee will be deducted from the profile’s earnings (if any) at the end of the month.  Any profile with a negative hosting fee balance may be locked and/or deleted.  Once a profile has been deleted, SubscribePlace agrees to waive any negative Hosting Fee balance. or more in a calendar month to receive a payout.

For example, if a profile owner sets a monthly fee of $8 for subscribers, then at checkout, subscribers will pay $9.99 per month – which includes SubscribePlace's $1.99 service fee.

Profile owners who make the $200 minimum or more get paid 2-3 business days after the last day of the calendar month. PayoutsFor profile owners who qualify for a payout, SubscribePlace will send a payout email within 2-3 business days after the end of the calendar month with a profile owner’s Revenue Share earnings. Payouts are based on the active subscriber number as measured on the last day of the calendar month. For important information about payout and revenue share details, please see our Terms of Service. Payouts within the USA are made via ACH transfer and SubscribePlace will pay for the transfer costs within the USA.
