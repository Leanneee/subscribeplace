SubscribePlace – General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – Compliance And Data Protection Policy

SubscribePlace Does Not Sell Any Data Or Advertisements. SubscribePlace Does Not Store IP Information.

Collection Of Data Is Used Only For Processing Credit Card Payments And Paying Profile Owners. This Policy Describes How Personal Data Is Collected And Stored To Meet GDPR Standards – And To Comply With All Applicable Law.

GDPR: The European Union General Data Protection Regulation (EU-GDPR) Was Designed To Protect EU Citizens’ Data Privacy And To Reshape The Way Organizations Across The Region Approach Data Privacy. The GDPR Aims Primarily To Give Control Back To Citizens Their Personal Data And To Simplify The Regulatory Environment For International Business By Unifying The Regulation Within The EU.

What Is Personal Data? “Personal Data” Is Defined In The GDPR As Any Information Relating To A Person Who Can Be Identified, Directly Or Indirectly, In Particular By Reference To An Identifier Such As A Name, An Identification Number, Location Data, Online Identifier Or To One Or More Factors Specific To The Physical, Physiological, Genetic, Mental, Economic, Cultural Or Social Identity Of That Person. So In Many Cases Online Identifiers Including IP Address, Cookies And So Forth Will Now Be Regarded As Personal Data If They Can Be (Or Are Capable Of Being) Without Undue Effort Linked Back To The Data Subject.

You can visit our website (www.subscribeplace.com) without giving away your personal information. SubscribePlace uses Google Analytics to understand the analytics behind our website. The information collected by Google Analytics is mostly anonymous traffic data including browser information, device information, language.

The collected information is used to provide an overview of how people are accessing and using the SubscribePlace website. It is not used for any additional purpose, such as to profile those who access our website. Once again, SubscribePlace does not sell any user data whatsoever. Moreover, SubscribePlace does not sell ads to users whatsoever. SubscribePlace is purely a subscription service.

(A). Purchasing a subscription

When you purchase a subscription, SubscribePlace collects the bare minimum information needed to comply with credit card identification and to satisfy information requirements to process credit card payments by a 3rd party service. Credit card numbers are not stored on our servers (as in line with PCI compliance laws).

(B) Payout Procedures

When you sign up to become a profile owners on SubscribePlace, there is certain data that needs to be collected in order for SubscribePlace to pay your bank account. Also, we are required by law to collect information related to tax purposes as mandated by federal law.

(C) Your rights

You have the right to request access to the information we have on you. You can do this by contacting us at [email protected] We will make sure to provide you with a copy of the data we have about you. In order to comply with your request, we may ask you to verify your identity. We will fulfill your request by sending your copy electronically, unless the request expressly specifies a different method. For any subsequent access request, we may charge you an administrative service fee.

(D). Information Correction & Deletion

If you believe that the information we have about you is incorrect, you are welcome to contact us so we can update it and keep your data accurate. Any data that is no longer needed for credit card billing purposes will be deleted. If at any point you wish for SubscribePlace to delete information about you, please contact us at [email protected]

(E). This Privacy Policy is applicable to the services offered by SubscribePlace directly via our website. Our website may contain links to other websites. Once redirected to another website, this Policy is no longer applicable.

This version of the Privacy Policy is effective from June 2018.

(F) We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy. We constantly review our Privacy Policy and strive towards making it better. All changes to our Privacy Policy will be available on this website.

(G) You can contact us at any time to: 1) Request access to information that SubscribePlace has about you; 2) Correct any information that SubscribePlace has about you; 3) Delete information that SubscribePlace has about you
